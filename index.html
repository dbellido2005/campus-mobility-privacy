Campus Mobility - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Campus Mobility is a ride-sharing app for Claremont Colleges students. We collect:

  • Email address (for account creation and verification)
  • College affiliation
  • Ride request information (pickup/destination locations, timing)
  • Profile information (name, year, major - optional)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect you with other students for ride sharing
  • Send ride notifications and updates
  • Improve our service

Information Sharing

We only share your information with:

  • Other students participating in your rides
  • Uber (for price estimates and ride coordination)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.
